From Katipunan or Marikina to School of Labor and Industrial Relations (SOLAIR) and National College of Public Administration and Governance (NCPAG)

Marikina

From Marikina, alight at Aurora Blvd corner Katipunan and walk towards the jeepney terminal under the Katipunan Flyover.

LRT2-Katipunan

If you’re coming from the LRT-2 Katipunan Station, take the North Exit on the same side as St. Bridget, opposite PSBA. Walk towards the jeepney terminal under the Katipunan Flyover.

Katipunan Jeepney Terminal

There are two queues calling UP at the terminal. Make sure you ride the jeep which goes inside the campus (the jeep line closer to Ministop). The other jeep line (farther from Ministop) goes to Tandang Sora passing through MWSS and will not go inside UP. To be sure, ask the jeepney barker before boarding.

Magsaysay Ave Gate

The UP-Katipunan jeep enters UP Diliman through the gate opposite MWSS.

GT-Toyota

Upon entry to UP, you'll see the GT Toyota Building(white) on the left.

Romulo Hall Waiting Shed

Alight the jeep at Romulo Hall. This is the first intersection the UP-Katipunan jeep encounters once inside UP. Go to the Romulo Hall Waiting Shed along E. Ma. Guerrero Road, then ride the next Ikot Jeep that passes by.

Ilang-Ilang Residence Hall

While at E. Ma. Guerrero St., the jeep will pass by Ilang-Ilang Residence Hall before turning left at Laurel St.

Parish of the Holy Sacrifice

While at Laurel St., the jeep passes by the Parish of the Holy Sacrifice (UP Chapel).

UP Shopping Center

You will also see the UP Shopping Center along Laurel St.

Kalayaan Residence Hall

The jeep will then turn left at Roces St. where you'll see the Kalayaan Residence Hall.

Molave Residence Hall

The jeep then turns right at Magsaysay St. You’ll see Molave Residence Hall at Roces cor Magsaysay St.

Ang Bahay ng Alumni

While at Magsaysay St, you'll see the jeep pass by Ang Bahay ng Alumni.

When you reach Ylanan Hall (College of Human Kinetics) or more commonly known as the Gym, the Ikot Jeep will make a left turn to E. Jacinto St.

SOLAIR (School of Labor and Industrial Relations)

SOLAIR will be the first building you see on your right after the jeep makes a turn at E. Jacinto.

NCPAG (National College of Public Administration and Governance)

The NCPAG complex can be reached on foot from SOLAIR or you may tell the Ikot Jeep driver to take you inside the complex.



If you informed the driver prior, instead of turning left at the first corner (towards E. Jacinto St) after passing by Ylanan Hall, the jeep turns left at the second corner (past the Archery Range) going through a street that leads to the NCPAG Complex.
