Katipunan Jeepney Terminal

From Marikina, alight at Aurora Blvd corner Katipunan and walk towards the jeepney terminal under the Katipunan Flyover.

If you’re coming from the LRT-2 Katipunan Station, take the North Exit on the same side as St. Bridget, opposite PSBA. Walk towards the jeepney terminal under the Katipunan Flyover.

There are two queues calling UP at the terminal. Make sure you ride the jeep which goes inside the campus (the jeep line closer to Ministop). The other jeep line (farther from Ministop) goes to Tandang Sora and will not go inside UP. To be sure, ask the barker before boarding.

The UP-Katipunan jeep enters UP Diliman through the gate opposite MWSS (Manila Water), the Magsaysay Gate.

The jeep is now on the Magsaysay Ave Road where you can spot the GT-Toyota Asian Center on your left.

Get off at the Romulo Hall on the first intersection after the UP-Katipunan jeep enters the campus. Walk to the waiting shed nearby along F. Ma. Guerrero Road.

Law Center (Bocobo Hall)



The buildings you see in front of the waiting shed form the Law Complex. Cross F. Ma. Guerrero Road, and walk past the Law Complex parking lot. The Bocobo Hall entrance should be at your left.

Malcolm Hall (College of Law)



To get to Malcolm Hall from the waiting shed, cross F. Ma. Guerrero Road then keep walking towards the Academic Oval or the Sunken Garden. When you reach F. Ma. Guerrero Rd corner Osmeña (marked by a triangular island), walk to your right where you will see the building’s entrance.
